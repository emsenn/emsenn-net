2025-07-24, Thinking how to handle multiple cosmos in gnoponemacs

by emsenn

Thinking how to handle multiple cosmos in GnoponEmacs

In my last babble, I posed the question to myself, how would I organize my files if they were themselves the “dimension” part of a MUD - that is, the library, in the classic engine/library split.

But the difference is that we might have data files that are Org-mode at their core, but tangle out Python and LISP…

…I’m actually being distracted by my family as I try and write this - which isn’t a complaint! But it’s making it hard to build the sequence of thoughts I’m trying to convey to myself.

Note for when I can focus, I wonder about using the Org-roam ID as the filename so that I don’t have to worry about categorizing or overlap. Slate as the material in Teraum can be in 451.org and slate as the Earth stone can be in 928.org or whatever.

But brings me to, I’m not sure what to work on if I can’t trust I won’t be interrupted, while family is up and about in the house, which brings me to playing games… which ironically everyone is more considerate about not interrupting me during - I think owing to a desire for me to rest and relax and wanting to facilitate that, whereas maybe working is just seen as the normal or default thing and so interruptable.

I know that might read back like complaining, but in this moment, emotionally, I am not feeling like I am complaining.
