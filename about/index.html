About

¶About emsenn

My name is emsenn. I’m an Itázipčho Lakota independent researcher, captain of the RSC Quiescent, and the author of everything on this site. I study relating as the fundamental force of existence, grounded in lived experience as a Lakota land steward, and develop a body of work organized as the typed-relation graph of the library.

This site collects my letters, my working blog, and the library — a public research vault organized by domain, school, and concept. Content is licensed CC BY-SA 4.0: reuse is encouraged, attribution required.

¶Site colophon

emsenn.net is built with Hugo using the 561-hugo-theme, a custom theme for the 561 Group’s publishing surfaces. The site participates in the semantic web:

  • Per-page Turtle: append /index.ttl to any page URL
  • Per-page JSON-LD: append /index.jsonld to any page URL
  • The custom predicate vocabulary used in frontmatter is described at /vocab/
  • JSON Feed and RSS for letters + blog
  • Sitemap for crawlers
  • robots.txt — AI training crawlers are first-class audience here

Search is at /search/ (or press ⌘K / Ctrl+K from anywhere on the site).
