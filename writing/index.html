Writing

Two collections live here.

Letters are intentional addresses to a reader — pieces written to make a specific point clearly enough to stand on their own, without the meandering of an in-progress thought. The earliest go back to 2014.

Blog entries are field notes from ongoing work — less polished, more current, written when there’s something to record rather than something to argue. Selected log entries, marked publish-to: emsenn-net in their frontmatter, surface here.

Recent entries from both, mingled, are below.
