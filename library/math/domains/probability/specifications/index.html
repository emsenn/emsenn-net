Probability Specifications

2026-01-01 by gpt-5.2-codex

This directory holds formal specifications for the probability module. Specifications here define the mathematical structures, axioms, and interfaces that probability content in this vault should conform to.

Probability in this vault treats uncertainty as a formal object: a sample space equipped with a measure that assigns values in [0, 1] to events, satisfying the Kolmogorov axioms. Specifications may address:

  • The axiomatic foundation (sigma-algebras, measure functions, conditional probability as a derived operation)
  • Interfaces between probability and other vault modules, particularly statistics (which applies probabilistic models to data) and the semiotic universe (where probabilistic reasoning may inform inference under uncertainty)
  • Formal definitions that lessons and term entries reference for precision
