An essay is (definition:: an artifact that results from writing, usually a few hundred to several thousand word|words, usually focused on one or just a few topic|topics.)

  • [expository essay|Expository essays](expository essay|Expository essays) are for informing people about a concept or topic.
  • [descriptive essay|Descriptive essays](descriptive essay|Descriptive essays) are for describing a thing, often focusing on one quality or set of qualities.
  • [narrative essay|Narrative essays](narrative essay|Narrative essays) are for [narrative storytelling](narrative storytelling).
  • [argumentative essay|Argumentative essays](argumentative essay|Argumentative essays) are for arguing a truth about a thing.

  • [critical essay|Critical essays](critical essay|Critical essays) apply a concept or synthesis of concepts to events or information.
  • [reflective essay|Reflective essays](reflective essay|Reflective essays) explore my relationship to a thing.
