Landback Publication Bundle

2026-03-04
  • reading|Read LANDBACK Publication Bundle [created:: 2023-12-06 priority:: lowest ]

Available as a limited print run, Issue 2 of the LANDBACK Magazine features over 140 pages of writing, photography and art on topics ranging from: the need for an Independent Hawaiʻi in the wake of the Lahaina Fire, producing in the era of Indigenous filmmaking and overcoming disconnection through motherhood and ceremony. We are pleased to welcome you back to the page where we are holistically exploring what LANDBACK means for Indigenous Peoples across Turtle Island and beyond. 

About the book: 

Let’s Get The Land Back: A Toolkit to Restore Our Relations, is meant to serve as a tangible roadmap, source of inspiration, and resource guide for getting land back to tribes. In these pages you’ll find practical considerations on how to give and receive Land Back as well as resources to address our own internal issues of fractionated land. We are honored to be able to share the stories of people, communities, and Nations who are leading the way and offer strategies to support your vision and actions.
