Hypertensor product

2026-03-04

The hypertensor product is a monoidal operation on recognition fibers induced by trace concatenation in the trace category. When two traces t1t_1 and t2t_2 are concatenated to form t1⋅t2t_1 \cdot t_2, the hypertensor product combines their fiber algebras into a single fiber algebra for the concatenated trace.

Axiom A4 of the QCHTTopos specification defines this operation. The hypertensor product gives the topos its characteristic “hypertensor” structure — the monoidal geometry induced by trace composition.
