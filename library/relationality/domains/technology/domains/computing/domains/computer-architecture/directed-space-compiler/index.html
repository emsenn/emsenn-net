Directed Space compiler

Directed Space Compiler Architecture

Generates the directed spaces, flows, and stratified sites consumed by runtime, analytics, and reasoning modules.

Dependencies

  • Directed Homotopy Simulation: supplies flow/nucleus mechanics and tower coherence checks for oriented paths.
  • Directed Stratified Site Simulation: provides strata definitions, coverage, and tower templates for each module family.
  • Topology Integration Simulation: consumes compiled artifacts; this service validates that emitted bundles satisfy the integration rules.
  • Measurement Fabric Simulation: exports compiler diagnostics (coherence checks, coverage errors) for monitoring/policy use.
  • Traceability & Knowledge Service: records which acts/specs justify each published topology bundle.

Components

  1. Strata Registry.
    Materializes the strata catalog with their discipline/filter settings and exposes APIs to register new module strata.

  2. Coverage Builder.
    Evaluates discipline/filter rules to compute coverage families, ensuring they are stable under directed morphisms before publishing them to consumers.

  3. Homotopy Engine.
    Maintains the directed flow/nucleus operators, computes reachable components, and records tower coherence diagnostics.

  4. Export Layer.
    Emits compiled site bundles (objects, morphisms, towers) for the sheaf /infinity-site modules and for runtime scheduling constraints, tagging each export with traceability IDs and publishing health metrics via the measurement fabric.

Invariants

  • Flow and nucleus must commute for every published stratum.
  • Coverage families remain closed under pullback and composition.
  • Tower projections agree with underlying strata definitions and are traceable to the underlying acts/specs.
