stewardship (tag)

The stewardship tag marks concepts that focus on caring for, revising, curating, or governing semiotic structures and knowledge artifacts over time.

Use this tag when a document is about responsibilities, roles, and actions that keep a semiotic universe healthy, legible, and useful.
