Updating Beliefs with Bayes

2026-01-01

Audience: beginners learning how evidence changes probability.

Learning goal: describe Bayes’ rule as updating prior beliefs with evidence.

Bayes’ rule combines a prior belief about an event with new evidence to produce an updated probability. It is useful when evidence is imperfect or uncertain.

Check for understanding: What role does new evidence play in Bayes’ rule?
