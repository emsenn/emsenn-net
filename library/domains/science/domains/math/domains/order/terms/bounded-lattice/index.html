2026-03-30 A lattice with a top element ⊤ (join of everything) and a bottom element ⊥ (meet of everything).

A bounded lattice is a lattice with a top element ⊤\top and a bottom element ⊥\bot:

  • ⊥≤a\bot \leq a for all aa (bottom is below everything)
  • a≤⊤a \leq \top for all aa (top is above everything)

Every complete lattice is bounded. Every finite lattice is bounded.

The four-valued bilattice is bounded under both its orderings: truth-bottom is ff, truth-top is tt; knowledge-bottom is ⊥\bot, knowledge-top is ⊤\top.

The twist product construction requires a bounded distributive lattice as input.
