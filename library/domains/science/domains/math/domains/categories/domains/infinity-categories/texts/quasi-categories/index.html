Quasi-Categories

2025-12-31

A quasi-category is a simplicial set in which every inner horn has a filler. This condition ensures that higher composition exists up to coherent homotopy.

Intuition

  • Vertices represent objects.
  • Edges represent 1-morphisms.
  • Higher simplices encode higher morphisms and their coherence data.

Quasi-categories are a widely used model for infinity categories because they translate higher categorical structure into combinatorial data.
