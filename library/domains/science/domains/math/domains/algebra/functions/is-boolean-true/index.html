is-boolean-true cascades every boolean-element claim in the substrate into a parallel is-boolean-true-result fiber with the same value, preserving the structured channels that carry each contribution. Reading the result fiber at a subject is the act of asking “is this resource boolean-true?” — the answer is the witnessing configuration of channels at that point.

Operation

The frontmatter declares one specialization morphism through nested mapping syntax: the top-level mapping boolean-element: is interpreted as a claim with boolean-element as subject, producing (boolean-element, specialization, is-boolean-true-result)(\text{boolean-element},\ \text{specialization},\ \text{is-boolean-true-result}). By Theorem 2 of the adjoint structure of predication paper, specialization is a bilattice homomorphism: every claim in the boolean-element fiber derives a claim at the same (subject,structured-object)(\text{subject}, \text{structured-object}) point in the is-boolean-true-result fiber, with the same bilattice value.

The cascade is standing. Every boolean-element message that flows through the substrate populates the is-boolean-true-result fiber automatically — there is no separate “invocation” step.

Reading the result

To find out whether a resource XX is boolean-true, walk the substrate’s response geometry for claims matching (X,is-boolean-true-result,∗)(X, \text{is-boolean-true-result}, *). Each match is one witnessing channel — its structured object traces back through the morphism cascade to the original message claims that established the boolean evidence. The set of channels at the subject is the answer: each channel records one path of evidence, and the caller reads them as the witnessing configuration rather than collapsing them into a primitive label.

Composition with boolean-and

boolean-and builds new boolean-element channels by fusing pairs of existing ones at the same subject. Those derived channels are themselves boolean-element claims, so the is-boolean-true cascade picks them up and propagates them into is-boolean-true-result. The two functions compose: boolean-and enriches the boolean-element fiber, and is-boolean-true mirrors that enrichment into the result fiber.
