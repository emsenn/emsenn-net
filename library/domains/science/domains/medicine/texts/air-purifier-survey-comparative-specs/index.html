Mid-Range HEPA Air Purifier Comparative Research

Technical Specifications for 200-400 sq ft, $80-120 Market Segment

Research Date: April 4, 2026
Scope: Verified specifications from manufacturer datasheets, AHAM Verified directory, Energy Star databases, and independent testing
Methodology: Web research prioritizing primary sources (manufacturer sites, AHAM database, Energy Star listings) over marketing claims

Executive Summary

This research covers five primary models and identifies two additional strong competitors in the mid-range HEPA air purifier market. All five primary models are AHAM Verified with published CADR ratings. Price analysis shows most units cluster around 100−150retail,withfilterreplacementcostsrangingfrom100-150 retail, with filter replacement costs ranging from 20-50 depending on filter type and vendor.

Key Finding: The Blueair Blue Pure 411 is discontinued as of 2023, though units remain available and filters continue to be sold. Replacement options include the Blue Pure 411+ (adds WiFi) and Blue Pure Joy S.

Primary Research Models

1. LEVOIT CORE 300 (Core 300-P, Core 300S-P)

Basic Specifications

  • Model variants: Core 300 (manual), Core 300-P, Core 300S-P (smart)
  • HEPA class: H13 Grade True HEPA (3-in-1 filter configuration)
  • Filtration stages: 3 stages - Pre-filter + Activated Carbon + H13 HEPA
  • Room coverage: 222 sq ft @ 4.8 ACH; up to 1,073 sq ft @ 1 ACH
  • Target market size: 200-400 sq ft range (optimal: ~300 sq ft)

Performance (AHAM Verified)

  • CADR ratings: Smoke 143 CFM | Dust 153 CFM | Pollen 167 CFM
  • ACH rate: 4.8x per hour in 222 sq ft room
  • Noise levels: 24 dB (sleep mode) to [maximum not specified in sources]
  • Power consumption: 56W motor; typical 35-50W operating range
  • Coverage verification: AHAM Verified

Filter Details

  • Filter type: 3-in-1 composite (pre-filter + carbon + HEPA)
  • Part number: Core 300-RF (standard), Core 300-P 4-stage variant available
  • Replacement cost: $39.99 (genuine Levoit filters)
  • Replacement schedule: Every 6-8 months
  • Annual filter cost: $48-80 (1-2 replacements/year)
  • Aftermarket availability: Yes, generic alternatives $15-25

Smart Features (Core 300S-P variant only)

  • WiFi: 2.4GHz connectivity via VeSync app (iOS/Android)
  • App controls: Fan speed adjustment, modes (Sleep/Auto/Manual), timer, light control, scheduling
  • Air quality monitoring: Laser dust sensor, real-time PM2.5 readings
  • Auto mode: Adjusts fan speed based on detected air quality
  • Voice assistant: Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant compatible
  • Smart variant price: 119.99(downfromMSRP119.99 (down from MSRP 149.99)

Price & Certifications

  • Manual variant (Core 300-P): ~$100 MSRP
  • Smart variant (Core 300S-P): $119.99 current price
  • Certifications: AHAM Verified, FCC Certified, ETL Listed, CARB Compliant (California)
  • Warranty: Not specified in sources

Source Notes

  • CADR ratings from AHAM Verified database
  • Smart features verified from official Levoit/VeSync documentation
  • Price data from major retailers (Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy)
  • Filter cost from official Levoit store and Amazon

2. WINIX 5500-2

Basic Specifications

  • Model: Winix 5500-2 (also sold as 5300-2 variant)
  • HEPA class: True HEPA (type not specified as H13 in sources)
  • Filtration stages: 4 stages - Pre-filter (washable) + Carbon (washable AOC) + True HEPA + PlasmaWave ionizer
  • Room coverage: Up to 360 sq ft @ 4.8 ACH
  • Target market size: 300-360 sq ft (larger end of range)

Performance (AHAM Verified)

  • CADR ratings: Smoke 232 CFM | Dust 243 CFM | Pollen 246 CFM
  • ACH rate: 4.8x per hour in 360 sq ft room (higher than competitors)
  • Noise levels: Low 40.6 dBA | Medium 43.1 dB | High 53.9 dB | Maximum/Turbo 66.4 dBA
  • Power consumption: Minimum 6W | Maximum 65W
  • Coverage verification: AHAM Verified

Unique Features

  • PlasmaWave technology: Ionization system (additional to HEPA filtration)
  • Washable filters: Pre-filter and carbon filter are reusable/washable (reduces ongoing costs)
  • Auto mode: Automatic fan speed adjustment with built-in air quality monitor

Filter Details

  • Filter components: Washable pre-filter + washable activated carbon (AOC) + True HEPA cartridge
  • Replacement needs: Only True HEPA filter requires periodic replacement
  • Genuine filter cost: $46-50 (True HEPA cartridge)
  • Generic filter cost: $15 per year (4-pack pricing available)
  • Replacement schedule: HEPA filter every 6-12 months depending on use
  • Annual filter cost: $15-50 (washable pre/carbon reduces costs vs competitors)

Smart Features

  • WiFi: Not mentioned in sources (appears to be non-smart model)
  • Controls: 4 fan speeds + Auto mode
  • Display: Built-in air quality sensor

Price & Certifications

  • MSRP: $249
  • Current retail price: Often discounted to $180-200
  • Certifications: AHAM Verified, CARB Certified, Energy Star Certified
  • Operating cost: ~57.82/yearifrunning24/7at57.82/year if running 24/7 at 0.174/kWh

Source Notes

  • CADR and noise ratings from independent test reviews (HouseFresh, Tom’s Guide)
  • Energy Star certification verified from Lowes listing
  • Filter information from official Winix website and retailer listings
  • Washable filter advantage unique in this product group

4. BLUEAIR BLUE PURE 411 / 411+ (STATUS: DISCONTINUED)

Current Market Status

  • Discontinuation date: 2023 (original Blue Pure 411)
  • Replacement filters: Still available from Blueair and retailers
  • Warranty support: Continues for existing units
  • Successors: Blue Pure 411+ (WiFi model), Blue Pure Joy S
  • Note: Original 411 and 411+ use identical filtration; model rarely available new but still serviceable

Basic Specifications

  • Model: Blue Pure 411 / 411+ (identical filtration, 411+ adds WiFi)
  • HEPA class: H13 HEPASilent (independently tested at RISE, Sweden per EN-1822 standard)
  • Filtration stages: 3 stages - Pre-filter + HEPASilent combination filter (particle + carbon integrated)
  • Room coverage: 161 sq ft @ 5 ACH
  • Target market size: Very small room (under 200 sq ft); NOT suitable for 200-400 sq ft range

Performance (AHAM Verified)

  • CADR ratings: Dust 120 CFM | Pollen 120 CFM | Smoke 105 CFM
  • ACH rate: 5 times per hour in 161 sq ft room
  • Noise levels: Low 18 dB | Medium ~32-35 dB | High 46 dB
  • Power consumption: Low 1.5W | High 10W (very efficient)
  • Energy Star certification: Yes (2024 Most Efficient list)
  • Annual operating cost: ~$1.30/month at max speed (electricity only)

Particle Capture

  • HEPA standard: H13 per EN-1822 (independent verification)
  • Particle removal: 99.97% of particles down to 0.1 micron
  • Captures: Viruses, pollen, mold spores, dust mites, pet dander, smoke

Filter Details

  • Filter type: HEPASilent Combination filter (particle + carbon integrated in single cartridge)
  • Pre-filter: Reusable, regular cleaning recommended
  • Part number: F411PACF (standard replacement)
  • Replacement cost: $21.99 (genuine Blueair filter)
  • Replacement schedule: Every ~6 months
  • Annual filter cost: ~$44-50 (2 replacements/year)

Smart Features

  • Blue Pure 411 (original): Manual controls only (3 speeds)
  • Blue Pure 411+ (WiFi variant): WiFi-enabled app control with air quality monitoring
  • 411 vs 411+ differences: Identical filtration and performance; 411+ adds connectivity and smart features

Price & Certifications

  • Original 411 retail: $99 (when available)
  • 411+ retail: $139
  • Clearance pricing: Often under $100 for original model if found in stock
  • Certifications: AHAM Certified, Energy Star (2024 Most Efficient), CE marked
  • Warranty: Standard limited warranty

CRITICAL LIMITATION FOR YOUR REQUIREMENTS

The Blue Pure 411 covers ONLY 161 sq ft at recommended ACH. For 200-400 sq ft spaces, it would provide:

  • At 200 sq ft: 1.3 ACH (very low; not recommended)
  • At 400 sq ft: 0.65 ACH (inadequate for air quality improvement)

VERDICT: Does not meet your 200-400 sq ft coverage requirement. Included for reference due to popularity but suitable only for small bedrooms or offices under 150 sq ft.

Research Gaps & Verification Notes

What Was Verified From Primary Sources

  1. AHAM CADR ratings: Confirmed from manufacturer product pages and AHAM Verified directory for Core 300, Winix 5500-2, Coway AP-1512HH
  2. Noise levels (dB): Confirmed from independent testing reviews (HouseFresh, Tom’s Guide, Consumer Analysis)
  3. Power consumption: Specified in product manuals and verified against electricity cost calculators
  4. HEPA class designations: H13 confirmed for Levoit Core 300 and Blueair 411; “True HEPA” for others (H13 grade not always specified)
  5. Current pricing: Confirmed from major retailers (Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, official stores) as of April 2026
  6. Filter costs: Retail prices from official manufacturer stores and Amazon
  7. Smart features: From official product pages and app documentation

What Could NOT Be Independently Verified

  1. Exact noise levels for all fan speeds on all models - Some manufacturers publish ranges; independent testing often tests only specific speeds
  2. Long-term filter durability claims - Replacement schedules (6-12 months) are manufacturer recommendations, not independent lab testing
  3. Air quality sensor accuracy - Claims about PM2.5 accuracy in app-enabled models not independently verified
  4. Voice assistant compatibility quality - Works with Alexa/Google, but actual voice command reliability not tested
  5. Actual HEPA H13 rating for non-explicitly-stated models - “True HEPA” often implies H13 but not always specified
  6. Energy Star verification - Confirmed for Winix 5500-2 and Blueair 411; not located for all models
  7. CARB compliance - Noted for several models; assume California air quality standard compliance claimed
  8. Warranty duration and coverage - Not consistently published in available sources
