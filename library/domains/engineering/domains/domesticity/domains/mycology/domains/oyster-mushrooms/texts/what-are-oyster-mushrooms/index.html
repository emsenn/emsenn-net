What Are Oyster Mushrooms?

2025-12-30 A plain-language overview of oyster mushrooms.

What they are

Oyster mushrooms are a group of edible fungi in the Pleurotus family. They grow on wood and break it down.

Why they are popular

  • Fast growing.
  • Tolerant of different substrates.
  • Good flavor and texture.

Life cycle in one paragraph

Spores start mycelium. Mycelium spreads through a food source. When conditions are right, it forms clusters of mushrooms (fruiting bodies).
