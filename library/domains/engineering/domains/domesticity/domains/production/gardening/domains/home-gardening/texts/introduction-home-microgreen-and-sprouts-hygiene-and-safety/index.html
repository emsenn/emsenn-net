Introduction to home microgreens and sprouts hygiene and safety

2026-01-01 Audience: beginners who want to avoid food safety problems.

Audience: beginners who want to avoid food safety problems.

Learning goal: list the key hygiene steps for safe microgreens and sprouts.

¶Clean tools and surfaces

Wash trays, jars, and lids with hot soapy water, then rinse well. Let them dry before the next batch.

¶Rinse and drain

For sprouts, rinse seeds and growing sprouts with clean water 2-3 times per day. Drain fully so water does not pool.

¶Watch for spoilage

Discard batches with off odors, slimy texture, or unusual discoloration. When in doubt, throw it out.

Check for understanding: Why is draining as important as rinsing for sprouts?
