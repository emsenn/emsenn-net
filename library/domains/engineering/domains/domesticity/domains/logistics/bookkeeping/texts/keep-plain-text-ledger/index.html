Keep Plain-Text Ledger

2025-12-31 Maintain a plain-text ledger with consistent account naming and balanced postings. Use when keeping books in text files.
  • Define a consistent chart of accounts and naming convention.
  • Record each transaction with date, description, and balanced postings.
  • Periodically validate the ledger for balance and formatting issues.
