What is 3D Printing?

2025-12-30 A plain-language overview of what 3D printing is.
Learning objectives
  • What is 3D Printing?

Assumed audience

  • Reading level: general adult.
  • Background: no engineering required.
  • Goal: understand the basic idea and why it matters.

Core idea

3D printing makes physical objects by adding material layer by layer from a digital design.

Common kinds

  • FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling): melted plastic is laid down in layers.
  • Resin printing: liquid resin is hardened by light in thin layers.

Why people use it

  • Rapid prototyping.
  • Custom parts or tools.
  • Small-batch manufacturing.

Limits to know

  • Prints have layer lines and weaker direction across layers.
  • Print quality depends on setup and material choice.
