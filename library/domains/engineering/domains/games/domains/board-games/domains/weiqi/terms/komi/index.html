komi

2026-03-09 Komi in [weiqi](../index.md) is the point compensation added to White's final score to offset Black's first-move advantage [@britgo2025default; @britgo2026whatisgo].

Komi in weiqi is the point compensation added to White’s final score to offset Black’s first-move advantage [@britgo2025default; @britgo2026whatisgo].

Komi matters because weiqi is asymmetrical at the start: Black moves first. Without compensation, that initiative would distort results across many even games. Komi is therefore a scoring adjustment, not a tactical rule inside local fights.
