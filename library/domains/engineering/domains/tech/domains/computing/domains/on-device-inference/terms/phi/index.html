Phi

2026-03-08

Phi is a family of small large language models from Microsoft Research, designed to demonstrate that high-quality training data can compensate for fewer parameters.

The current release, Phi-4 Mini (3.8B parameters), is notable for punching above its weight on reasoning benchmarks — competitive with models 3–10x its size on structured tasks. This is attributed to training heavily on synthetic data generated by larger models and curated educational content.

Phi models are particularly relevant to on-device inference because they are small enough for NPU acceleration. Microsoft’s Phi Silica is a Phi variant tuned specifically for Copilot+ PC NPUs, accessible through Windows APIs. Phi-4 Mini is also available as an ONNX model optimized for Qualcomm Snapdragon hardware.
