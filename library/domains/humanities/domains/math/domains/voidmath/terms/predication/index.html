Predication

2026-04-28 The directed assignment of a value at a triple of subject, predicate, and object — the standard-mathematical articulation of what relationality calls relating, expressed through the predication cell, the predicate category and its four canonical generators, and the bilattice-valued fiber algebra.

Predication is the assignment of a value at a directed triple — a subject, a predicate, and an object. It is the mathematical primitive that relating in the relationality framework becomes when expressed in the standard vocabulary of categorical logic and bilattice-valued algebra. The two terms name the same activity from different sides: relating is the activity in the philosophical commitment that relations are ontologically prior to entities; predication is the formal structure that activity has when written down with mathematics that already exists.

Predication algebra is not a bespoke invention. It is a synthesis of three established literatures:

  • The hyperdoctrines of Lawvere and Seely — categorical logic where the predicates form a category and the values live in fibers indexed by it.
  • The bilattice logic of Belnap, Ginsberg, and Fitting — values live in a bilattice with two parallel orders, knowledge and truth, allowing the four-cornered settlement (t\mathbf{t}, f\mathbf{f}, ⊤\top, ⊥\bot) instead of bivalent true/false.
  • The cloven Grothendieck fibrations of Hermida and Jacobs — the technical machinery that makes predicate substitution and quantification well-behaved across the predicate category.

What predication adds is the synthesis: a fibration whose fibers are bilattice-valued and whose base is structured as a predicate category, where the morphisms between predicates are generated by exactly four canonical classes corresponding to the four ways predicates can structurally relate.

¶The predication cell

The smallest unit of predication is the predication cell (s,p,o)∈Φ=E×Π×E(s, p, o) \in \Phi = E \times \Pi \times E, where EE is a set of entities and Π\Pi a set of predicates. The cell records the proposition predicate pp holds of subject ss in relation to object oo. A predication valuation assigns to each cell a value in a residuated bilattice L⋈\mathbf{L}^\bowtie — a position in the four-cornered settlement space: the value is what is recorded at that cell; the cell is where the recording happens.

The directed asymmetry of the cell — that the subject and object coordinates are structurally distinct, and the predicate is directional from the one to the other — is the formal expression of what the philosophical side calls lopsidedness or directional relating. There is no symmetric primitive underneath. The directed cell is the primitive; symmetric relations appear as the special case where a predicate equals its own converse.

¶The four-generator structure

Predicates do not exist in isolation; they are the objects of the predicate category C\mathcal{C}, whose morphisms record how predicates structurally relate to each other. Under a Sym(E)\mathrm{Sym}(E)-equivariance hypothesis (with ∣E∣≥3|E| \geq 3), every natural map between predication fiber algebras is generated by exactly four canonical classes:

  • Specialization — one predicate is a sub-predicate of another, formalized as a sub-support inclusion dom(q)↪dom(p)\mathrm{dom}(q) \hookrightarrow \mathrm{dom}(p).
  • Converse — the relational converse, induced by the swap (s,o)↦(o,s)(s, o) \mapsto (o, s).
  • Subject-projection — the diagonal collapse (s,o)↦(s,s)(s, o) \mapsto (s, s), extracting subject-only content.
  • Object-projection — the dual of subject-projection, (s,o)↦(o,o)(s, o) \mapsto (o, o).

That the generators are exactly four — and that this is a theorem about equivariance, not a stipulation — is the formal articulation of the four-cornered structure that relationality and its philosophical predecessors have been reaching for. Specialization is not a Boolean refinement; converse is not a symmetry; the projections are not abstraction operations. Each is a specific natural manipulation of the entity-pair index E×EE \times E, and together they exhaust the natural maps under equivariance.

¶Layers of structure

Predication is the unit; the surrounding structure builds in property-like layers (in the sense of Kelly–Lack: present-or-not, no choice involved, unique up to unique iso when present):

  1. The predication fiber algebra Ap=L⋈ E×EA_p = \mathbf{L}^{\bowtie\,E\times E} — the bilattice-valued values at all cells with predicate coordinate fixed at pp. The bilattice operations (∧t,∨t,∧k,∨k,∼)(\wedge_t, \vee_t, \wedge_k, \vee_k, \sim) lift pointwise.
  2. Predication fibration E↠C\mathcal{E} \twoheadrightarrow \mathcal{C} — the cloven Grothendieck fibration whose fibers are the fiber algebras and whose reindexing along each generator class is the cellwise map specified by that class. Equivalent to a pseudo-coalgebra for the Gray–Street fibration 2-comonad.
  3. Predication hyperdoctrine — adds two parallel adjoint quantifier systems, one in the knowledge order ≤k\leq_k and one in the truth order ≤t\leq_t, each with Frobenius reciprocity, Beck–Chevalley coherence, and comprehension. The two systems yield two derived equality predicates (classical and paraconsistent).

Beyond the hyperdoctrine layer sit settling-equipped structures (temporal content), Priestley duality (cohomology), and the applied programs that build on predication — knowledge presheaves, distributed settling systems, the spatiotemporal-knowledge construction. The layers stack: a hyperdoctrine is a fibration with extra properties; a settling-equipped hyperdoctrine is a hyperdoctrine with extra properties.

¶Why this is the earn

The relationality framework spent years searching for a math adequate to its commitments. Earlier formal attempts invented bespoke vocabulary — “void-excitations,” “bypass relations,” “fold successions,” “Recognition Movements,” “the four mathematical universes.” Those attempts groped at structure that turned out to be standard: bilattices were already in the literature, hyperdoctrines were already in the literature, fibrations were already in the literature, Priestley duality was already in the literature. The earn is the recognition that the math relationality wants is already mathematics — the synthesis is novel, but the components are not bespoke. Predication algebra is what falls out when the philosophical commitment is taken seriously and expressed in the language mathematicians already speak.
