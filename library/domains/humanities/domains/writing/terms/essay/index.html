An essay is (definition:: an artifact that results from writing, usually a few hundred to several thousand words, usually focused on one or just a few topics.)

Here’s a listing of various types of essays:

File definition
reflective essay -
argumentative essay an essay for arguing a truth about a thing

{ .block-language-dataview}

  • Expository essays are for informing people about a concept or topic.
  • Descriptive essays are for describing a thing, often focusing on one quality or set of qualities.
  • Narrative essays are for narrative storytelling.
  • Argumentative essays are for arguing a truth about a thing.
  • Analytical essays are for relating a concept or topic to events or information.
  • Comparative essays are for looking at what is similar and different between two or more things.
  • Critical essays apply a concept or synthesis of concepts to events or information.
  • Reflective essays explore my relationship to a thing.

Check the Essay Catalog if you want to see all the essays that are a part of my virtual mycelium.
