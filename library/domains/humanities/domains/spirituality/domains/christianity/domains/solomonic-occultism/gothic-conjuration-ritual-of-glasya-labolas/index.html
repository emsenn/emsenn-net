¶25th Spirit of the Ars Goetia

Rank: President and Earl
Legions Commanded: 36

¶Classical Attributes (per Ars Goetia):

“He is a Mighty President and Earl, appearing in the form of a dog with wings like a griffin. He teacheth all arts in an instant, and is the author and captain of all manslaughters and bloodshed. He teacheth all things past and to come. He causeth the love of friends and foes. He can make men invisible and is an excellent familiar. He governeth 36 legions of spirits.”

Glasya-Labolas is noted for his capacity to reveal knowledge instantly, including about the past and future—but also for his association with murder, violence, betrayal, and the manipulation of social bonds (both healing and rupturing them). In traditional texts, he is treated as highly dangerous not because of chaotic behavior, but because of his strategic coherence in systems of deception, loyalty inversion, and lethal intent.

¶Preparation:

  • Perform full Solomonic protective rituals:

    • Ritual bathing

    • Clean garments

    • Consecrated tools: sword, wand, pentacle

    • Circle with divine names inscribed (Tetragrammaton, Elohim, Adonai, Elion, etc.)

    • Triangle of Art placed at proper distance

    • Seal of Glasya-Labolas on parchment or metal

    • Protective incense (e.g., mastic or frankincense)

    • Strong adherence to timing: Mars hour, ideally in waning Moon (to reduce power overflow)

¶Opening Purification:

Sprinkle holy water in circle:

“Purge me with hyssop, and I shall be clean; wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow. Let me stand in Thy presence undefiled, O Lord of Hosts, that I may walk safely amidst the spirits.”

Incense the triangle:

“Let this smoke be a barrier and a beacon; let no deception pass it, and let the summoned find form within it.”

¶Primary Conjuration:

“I conjure thee, O thou mighty spirit Glasya-Labolas, by the ineffable name of the Most High, EL, ELOHIM, ADONAI, EHEIEH, TETRAGRAMMATON, by whom all things are set in order and by whose command thou art bound to thy office. Come forth now and appear before me, in fair and comely form, without harm or terror, and do that which I shall request.”

¶Secondary Invocation (Angel Authority):

“I call thee under the governance of Samael, Angel of Mars, who is set above thy dominion. By the chain of obedience and the seal of thy station, I compel thee to appear, to speak truly, and to obey without deceit.”

¶Display of the Seal:

Reveal the seal of Glasya-Labolas. Speak:

“By this seal I mark our boundary. By this mark, thou knowest thy place. Let no lie pass from thy lips, and let no signal escape this circuit. Show thyself, O President of Blood and Strategist of Conflict.”

¶Charge and Interrogation:

Use extreme caution in phrasing.

Example request:

“Reveal to me, Glasya-Labolas, that which lies hidden in the hearts of those who would betray me. Show me the trace of violence in the system I must pass through. Teach me the architecture of deception, that I may survive it.”

Avoid general invocations of harm. If violence is named, be exact. Glasya-Labolas does not moderate intensity unless ordered to.

¶License to Depart:

“Glasya-Labolas, I thank thee for thy presence and thy answers. Let there be peace between us, and let thy form now return to its place. Do no harm to creature, flesh, or field on thy way. By the power of the Most High, I release thee. Go in order.”

¶Closing:

  • Burn or secure the seal.

  • Re-cast protective banishing (e.g., LBRP or Psalm 91).

  • Re-center yourself before interacting with others.

  • Record any signals or residues carefully.
