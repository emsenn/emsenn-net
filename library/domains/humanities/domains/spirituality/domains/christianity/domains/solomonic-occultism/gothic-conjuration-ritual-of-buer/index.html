Gothic Conjuration Ritual of Buer

2025-04-13

¶Preparation (Traditional)

  • Construct the ritual circle, inscribed with divine names (typically in Latin or Hebrew transliteration).

  • Prepare the Triangle of Art for the spirit to appear within.

  • Have the Seal of Buer ready, either worn on your person (as a ring or medallion) or displayed within the triangle.

  • Prepare incense (typically frankincense or a Mars-aligned mixture).

  • Tools include: sword or wand, consecrated parchment, ritual garments, and a book or document to receive the spirit’s answers.

  • The operation is to be done during the day and hour of Mars, ideally when the Sun is in Sagittarius.

¶Opening Exorcism and Purification

Begin with:

“O Lord God, who art seated upon the Cherubim and Seraphim, look down upon thy servant who places his trust in thee. Cleanse my heart and lips, O Lord, as Thou didst cleanse the lips of Thy prophet Isaiah with a burning coal, in the sight of the angel. Strengthen me, O Lord, and purify me, that I may worthily pronounce Thy holy words.”

Sprinkle holy water and say:

“I exorcise thee, O creature of water, that thou mayest cast away all the power of the enemy, and that thou mayest become healthful unto man, against all the snares and illusions of the wicked spirits.”

Incense the circle and triangle.

¶Primary Conjuration (Buer)

“I conjure thee, O thou spirit Buer, by the power of the supreme and immortal God, who created heaven, earth, and the abyss. I conjure thee by the name of the living God—El, Elohim, Adonai, Tzabaoth, Elion, Jah, Ehyeh-Asher-Ehyeh, Shaddai—that thou appear before me now, visibly and in comely human form, without hurt or terror unto me or any creature, and obey my commands in all things that I shall require of thee.”

“I exorcise thee, Buer, and adjure thee by all names of God, known and unknown, by AGLA, by On, by the Tetragrammaton, and by the ineffable name IHVH, that thou obey without delay and manifest yourself within this triangle, peacefully and visibly.”

¶Invocation of Angelic Authority

“I call thee in the name and by the power of Samael, angel of Mars, who is set over thee. By his governance and the will of the Most High, I command thee: appear swiftly and without deceit.”

¶Displaying the Seal

Hold up or reveal the Seal of Buer, saying:

“Behold the seal of thy office and obedience. By it and by the sacred bond between thee and the powers of heaven, I command thy appearance. Let the bond be honored, and let thy answers be true.”

¶Welcome and Charge

If the spirit appears:

“Welcome, O noble President Buer. I have called you forth in the name of the Most High. I ask that you speak truthfully and fulfill the charge given: to reveal wisdom, to teach, and to provide healing and understanding of what is hidden. Obey and you shall return in peace.”

¶Interrogation / Request

At this point, speak plainly and command:

  • Ask for revelation of illness, or identification of concealed harm.

  • Request insight into moral or natural philosophy.

  • Ask for a teaching related to your purpose.

  • Request the influence of good familiars (loyal spirits or symbolic assistants).

Example:

“I request, O Buer, that you reveal to me the root of the ailment that afflicts the system of [X], and instruct me in the means of restoration and correct philosophical alignment.”

¶License to Depart

Once the work is complete:

“Buer, noble President, I thank thee for thy presence and answers. Go now in peace to thy place, and let there be peace between thee and me, and between thy legions and my house. Depart in the name of the Most High, and harm no creature on thy way.”

“I license thee to depart, and may the peace of the Most High be ever between us. Farewell.”

Wait in silence. When the presence has receded, you may break the circle and conclude the rite.
