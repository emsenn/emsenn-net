Major arcana

2025-12-31

Each major arcana card lists a short interpretation. Upright and reversed are sketches to be adapted to context.

The Fool

Upright: beginnings, openness, risk. Reversed: hesitation, naivete, avoidance.

The Magician

Upright: will, skill, initiative. Reversed: manipulation, scattered focus.

The High Priestess

Upright: intuition, hidden knowledge. Reversed: blocked intuition, secrecy.

The Empress

Upright: nurture, growth, abundance. Reversed: stagnation, overindulgence.

The Emperor

Upright: structure, authority, stability. Reversed: rigidity, domination.

The Hierophant

Upright: tradition, teaching, institutions. Reversed: dogma, rebellion.

The Lovers

Upright: alignment, relationship, choice. Reversed: imbalance, discord.

The Chariot

Upright: drive, control, momentum. Reversed: loss of direction, conflict.

Strength

Upright: courage, patience, inner power. Reversed: self-doubt, frustration.

The Hermit

Upright: solitude, reflection, guidance. Reversed: isolation, withdrawal.

Wheel of Fortune

Upright: cycles, change, turning points. Reversed: delay, resistance to change.

Justice

Upright: balance, accountability, clarity. Reversed: imbalance, avoidance.

The Hanged Man

Upright: pause, new perspective, surrender. Reversed: stasis, stubbornness.

Death

Upright: endings, transition, release. Reversed: clinging, stagnation.

Temperance

Upright: balance, moderation, harmony. Reversed: excess, misalignment.

The Devil

Upright: attachment, constraint, shadow. Reversed: release, detachment.

The Tower

Upright: disruption, revelation, collapse. Reversed: delayed upheaval.

The Star

Upright: hope, renewal, calm. Reversed: doubt, discouragement.

The Moon

Upright: uncertainty, intuition, illusion. Reversed: clarity, fear easing.

The Sun

Upright: vitality, success, openness. Reversed: pessimism, dimmed joy.

Judgement

Upright: reckoning, awakening, decision. Reversed: avoidance, self-doubt.

The World

Upright: completion, integration, wholeness. Reversed: unfinished work.
