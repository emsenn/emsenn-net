Minor arcana

2025-12-31

Minor arcana interpretations combine suit themes with rank themes.

¶Suit themes

  • Wands: drive, growth, creative force.
  • Cups: emotion, relationships, receptivity.
  • Swords: thought, conflict, clarity.
  • Pentacles: material life, craft, stability.

¶Rank themes

  • Ace: beginnings, seed.
  • Two: balance, choice.
  • Three: expansion, collaboration.
  • Four: stability, rest.
  • Five: tension, challenge.
  • Six: adjustment, movement.
  • Seven: strategy, assessment.
  • Eight: momentum, focus.
  • Nine: fruition, endurance.
  • Ten: completion, burden.
  • Page: learning, curiosity.
  • Knight: action, pursuit.
  • Queen: mastery, nurture.
  • King: leadership, command.

¶Wands

  • Ace of Wands: beginnings of drive and creativity.
  • Two of Wands: choosing a direction for growth.
  • Three of Wands: expansion through collaboration.
  • Four of Wands: stable momentum and celebration.
  • Five of Wands: tension in creative forces.
  • Six of Wands: recognition and forward movement.
  • Seven of Wands: defensive strategy and assessment.
  • Eight of Wands: rapid motion and focus.
  • Nine of Wands: endurance under pressure.
  • Ten of Wands: completion with heavy burden.
  • Page of Wands: learning a new spark.
  • Knight of Wands: acting on creative pursuit.
  • Queen of Wands: nurtured confidence and mastery.
  • King of Wands: leading with vision and drive.

¶Cups

  • Ace of Cups: beginnings of emotion and connection.
  • Two of Cups: balance and mutual exchange.
  • Three of Cups: shared joy and collaboration.
  • Four of Cups: stability, rest, or disengagement.
  • Five of Cups: emotional tension and loss.
  • Six of Cups: movement through memory and care.
  • Seven of Cups: assessment of emotional options.
  • Eight of Cups: focused departure or pursuit.
  • Nine of Cups: fruition and emotional endurance.
  • Ten of Cups: completion of relational harmony.
  • Page of Cups: learning emotional expression.
  • Knight of Cups: acting on feeling or invitation.
  • Queen of Cups: nurtured empathy and depth.
  • King of Cups: leadership through steady feeling.

¶Swords

  • Ace of Swords: beginnings of clarity and resolve.
  • Two of Swords: balance or stalemate in thought.
  • Three of Swords: expansion into conflict or pain.
  • Four of Swords: stable rest and recovery.
  • Five of Swords: tension through conflict.
  • Six of Swords: movement toward calmer clarity.
  • Seven of Swords: strategy and careful assessment.
  • Eight of Swords: focused pressure or restriction.
  • Nine of Swords: endurance through worry.
  • Ten of Swords: completion through rupture.
  • Page of Swords: learning critical thinking.
  • Knight of Swords: acting with sharp pursuit.
  • Queen of Swords: mastery of clarity and honesty.
  • King of Swords: leadership through reason.

¶Pentacles

  • Ace of Pentacles: beginnings in material life.
  • Two of Pentacles: balancing resources.
  • Three of Pentacles: collaborative craft and growth.
  • Four of Pentacles: stability and holding.
  • Five of Pentacles: material tension or scarcity.
  • Six of Pentacles: adjustment through exchange.
  • Seven of Pentacles: assessment of growth.
  • Eight of Pentacles: focused practice and work.
  • Nine of Pentacles: fruition and independence.
  • Ten of Pentacles: completion and legacy.
  • Page of Pentacles: learning practical skills.
  • Knight of Pentacles: steady pursuit and effort.
  • Queen of Pentacles: nurtured stability and care.
  • King of Pentacles: leadership through material command.
