Governance

¶Governance

Governance encompasses the structures, processes, and principles through which collectives make decisions, exercise authority, and maintain accountability. This domain covers:

  • Authority structures: how power is organized and exercised
  • Decision-making: consensus, voting, deliberation, and choice mechanisms
  • Accountability: how responsibility is assigned and violations addressed
  • Traditions: both state-centric and non-Western frameworks
  • Institutions: the concrete forms governance takes
