Sociology

The study of human society — its institutions, structures, power relations, and the forces that produce and contest social order. The library’s sociological content is organized into domains that correspond to real disciplines, schools, lineages, and topics within the field. Each domain’s _index.md declares what kind of domain it is.

Teleology
The goal of sociology is to understand how social order is produced, maintained, and contested — how institutions, power relations, and collective action shape human life.
Method
The method of sociology is critical analysis of social structures and power relations, drawing on specific schools and lineages (Marxism, critical theory, feminism, Black radical tradition, anarchism, etc.) to examine how societies organize themselves and how those arrangements are challenged.
