Business

The study and practice of organizing resources, people, and capital to create and sustain commercial enterprises. Content here covers accounting, finance, marketing, operations, strategy, and the practical mechanics of running a business.

Teleology: To understand how enterprises are built, financed, operated, and sustained – from the structure of a balance sheet to the logic of a pricing strategy.

Method: Practical analysis of commercial operations – how money flows, how organizations are structured, how markets are entered, and how decisions are made under financial constraint.
