Sigmund’s Sat Stacker is a personal BTC-yield vault on the Base chain, created by [Sigmund Vexler](Sigmund Vexler) via the 0x3288…9b1a wallet on 2025-03-27. Built using DexFi, it launched with a projected APY of ~42%, sourced entirely from swap fees on Aerodrome—not emissions.

Description:
80% BTC, 20% USDC. Designed to farm stablecoin swaps and auto-compound yield into more Bitcoin—quietly, forever.

Allocation

  • 60% tBTC/cbBTC (Aerodrome CL, 0.01%)
  • 25% USDC/cbBTC (Aerodrome CL, 0.05%)
  • 15% tBTC/USDC (Aerodrome CL, 0.30%)

Cumulative exposure: 80% BTC, 20% USDC
USDC is used purely as yield-fuel—never as a hedge.

Strategy

This vault is designed to act as a BTC accumulator, not a generic yield farm.

  • Yield is generated from concentrated liquidity swap fees on Aerodrome.
  • Earnings are auto-compounded across the vault to maintain the fixed USD-denominated allocation.
  • This causes overperformance in stable-heavy pairs to be rebalanced into BTC-heavy pools over time.
  • The result is slow, reliable BTC stacking—without ever compromising core exposure.

Performance Fee:
5.5% of yield is taken as performance fee—1% to Sigmund Vexler, 4.5% to DexFi protocol.
