Metaphysics

Metaphysics

The Western branch of philosophy that asks what reality is made of at the deepest level. The library’s content here spans substance metaphysics (the dominant tradition from Aristotle through modernity), process philosophy (Whitehead’s alternative grounded in becoming rather than being), and ontology (the study of what exists and what it means to exist).
