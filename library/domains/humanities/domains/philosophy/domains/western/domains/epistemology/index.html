Epistemology

The Western branch of philosophy that studies knowledge. Rationalism, empiricism, standpoint epistemology, and social constructivism each give different answers to the questions: what is knowledge? how is it produced? what makes a belief justified?

Note: Indigenous and Buddhist epistemologies (Lakota relational epistemology, pramāṇa theory) have their own domains — they are not subfields of Western epistemology.
