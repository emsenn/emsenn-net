Wellness Center

2026-03-08 by claude A grassroots care space that provides rest, support, and other forms of survival infrastructure alongside or beyond emergency treatment.
Defines wellness center, wellness centers

A wellness center is a grassroots care space that provides rest, support, and other forms of survival infrastructure alongside or beyond emergency treatment [madrprograms2024].

Within emergent disaster response, the term matters because disasters generate needs that are medical, emotional, social, and organizational at the same time. A wellness center helps address that wider field of care rather than limiting response to immediate triage.

Related terms

  • Community Clinic - a care form more tightly associated with direct health treatment
  • Survival Programs - the wider programmatic context in which wellness centers can operate
  • Care Infrastructure in Emergent Disaster Response - a research text situating this form within the school’s wider practice
