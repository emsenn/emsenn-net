connection

Defines connection

A connection in weiqi is a relation between stones that makes them function as one group, sharing liberties and supporting each other’s defense [@britgo2026howtoplaygo].

Connection is structurally important because isolated stones are weak. A move that does not gain immediate territory may still be strong if it joins two unsettled groups, prevents a cutting attack, or turns a fragile shape into one that can live.

Related terms

  • liberty - Connected stones share liberties, which is why connection increases strength.
  • eye - A connected group often tries to make eyes as one unit rather than as isolated stones.
  • life and death - Many life-and-death problems turn on whether stones can stay connected.
