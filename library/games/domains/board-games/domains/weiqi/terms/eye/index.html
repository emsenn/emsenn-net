Defines eye

An eye in weiqi is an empty point or enclosed space inside a group’s shape that functions as an internal liberty and helps determine whether the group can live [@britgo2026howtoplaygo].

Eyes matter because they change the status of a group. A surrounded group with no secure eye shape may still be dead, while a well-connected group with two separate eyes is usually safe. This is why players often defend shape even when no immediate capture is on the board.
