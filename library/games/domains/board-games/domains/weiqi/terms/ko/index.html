ko

2026-03-09
Defines ko

Ko in weiqi is a repetition constraint that forbids a player from making the immediate recapture that would recreate the previous board position [@britgo2025default].

Ko matters because it prevents endless local loops while preserving the tactical possibility of capture. Once a ko appears, the fight usually stops being purely local: the blocked player must move elsewhere first, which can turn the rest of the board into leverage for the eventual return to the ko point.
