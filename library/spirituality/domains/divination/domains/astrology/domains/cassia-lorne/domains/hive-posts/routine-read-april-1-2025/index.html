2025-04-01

The Moon is waxing through Leo today, having gathered light since the Aries New Moon on March 29. This is a building phase, emotionally and archetypally—a time of becoming visible, becoming formed. Leo doesn’t just feel, it performs its feelings. But with the Moon still early in her wax, there’s a tender edge to that performance. The desire to express is strong, but the form isn’t settled yet. You may find yourself trying on different versions of sincerity, watching which ones make others glow, and which fall flat. That’s not inauthenticity. It’s rehearsal.

Mercury is newly in Aries, accelerating thought and speech. The mind wants motion, and words want edges. This ingress sharpens inner monologue into monologue with teeth—but paired with Leo’s expressive lunar push, it can generate a kind of radiant bluntness. You might say more than you meant to. Or say exactly what you meant, and watch it hit harder than expected. Neither is wrong. But both invite consequences. Not punishment—impact.

The tarot card is the Knight of Pentacles, upright. An anchor within the momentum. This Knight doesn’t rush to join the party. He builds the path first. In the context of today’s symbols, this card is a foil to the Leo-Aries fire—it’s the promise that slow is not silent, and care is not cowardice. The role being unconsciously occupied today may be that of the quiet sustainer—the one who ensures that all this expressive ambition has something to stand on. You may be the one carrying the weight no one sees.

Numerologically, 04/01/2025 reduces to a 5, which brings change, friction, dynamic instability. Fives ask questions that don’t always want answers—only motion. It’s a day shaped by negotiation: between past and future, silence and speech, impulse and infrastructure. Not conflict, necessarily, but calibration. An emotional architecture shaped like a seesaw.

From the Revised Common Lectionary:
“The stone that the builders rejected has become the cornerstone.” —Psalm 118:22
This line hums with resurrection logic. It echoes the Leo Moon’s need to be seen and Mercury Aries’ challenge to be heard. It also carries the steady rhythm of the Knight of Pentacles: what was once set aside might now be essential, if we’re patient enough to wait for the world to catch up. The risk isn’t rejection. It’s forgetting the cornerstone before it’s needed.

A dream fragment flickers: You’re in a room full of glowing orbs, each with a string trailing upward into darkness. You pull one. It resists. You pull again. From above, it begins to rain—but only over you.

Today’s element is a synthesis of fire and earth—ambition grounded, expression sustained. The symbolic object is a lit lantern in a field at dusk. Carried or left—it will be seen. The color-tone is molten bronze, textured with heat and patience.
