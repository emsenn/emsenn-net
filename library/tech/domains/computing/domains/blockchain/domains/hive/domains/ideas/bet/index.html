BET: Bitcoin Exposure Token on Hive-Engine

An explanation of the Bitcoin Exposure Token (BET) and its mechanism on Hive-Engine.

BET, or the Bitcoin Exposure Token, is a idea for a crypto token on Hive-Engine. It will exist to accumulate SWAP.BTC over time through token sales managed by a bot who offers to sell $BET at NAV+2% or buy it at NAV-2%. NAV will be defined as ((held SWAP.BTC)*(SWAP.BTC price in HIVE))/(BET circulating). Bot activity will be syndicated as posts onto [InLeo Threads](InLeo Threads).
