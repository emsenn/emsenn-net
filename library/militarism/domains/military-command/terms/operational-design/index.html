Operational Design

2026-03-08 The analytical framework that underpins planning: framing the operational environment as a system, defining the problem, and developing an operational approach.
Defines operational design
<p>Operational design is the analytical framework that underpins operational-level planning: it helps commanders and staffs <strong>frame the operational environment</strong>, <strong>define the problem</strong>, and <strong>develop an operational approach</strong> <a href="#ref-jointchiefs2011jp5" class="citation">[jointchiefs2011jp5]</a>.</p> <p>A concise way to hold operational design is: <em>before you schedule actions, you decide what system you are in, what must change in it, and what broad logic will make it change.</em></p>
