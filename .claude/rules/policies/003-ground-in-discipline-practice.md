Draw on established methods of each discipline rather than inventing ad hoc practices.


- When starting work in a discipline, check: do we have documented
  methods for this discipline? If not, the first task is research, not
  content creation.
- Methods should be encoded at the discipline or school level:
  `discipline/disciplines/school/methods/` or similar.
- External research is not optional. Every discipline module should
  cite foundational methodological texts.
- When our practice conflicts with established discipline practice,
  document why (as a decision record), don't silently diverge.
- The research cadence for any domain is: external research → encode
  as concepts/terms → compose into texts → develop curricula →
  implement as skills → assist with scripts → verify formally where
  possible.
